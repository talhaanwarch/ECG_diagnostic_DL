Description

The dataset is comprised of ECG records of 1937 distinct patients. EDAN SERIES-3 ECG device is used to collect this data.

The data is collected from following hospitals

  • Ch. Pervaiz Elahi Institute of Cardiology, Multan
  • Nishtar Medical University, Multan
  • Institution: Punjab Institute of Cardiology, Lahore

There are five categories in dataset

  • COVID-19
  • Abnormal Heartbeat
  • Myocardial Infarction
  • Previous History of MI,
  • Normal Person
